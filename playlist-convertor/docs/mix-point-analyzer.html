New Feature

Mix Point Analyzer

Automatically detect where to mix in and where to mix out of every track. Hot cues land straight on your CDJs — no manual prep needed.

How It Works

The Analysis Pipeline

Energy-based detection finds where the track's body begins and ends, snaps to the beat grid, and writes hot cues directly into your Rekordbox XML.

1

Load Audio

Track is loaded at 22050 Hz mono — same sample rate used for BPM and key detection. Tracks under 60 seconds are skipped automatically.

2

Compute Energy Envelope

RMS energy is calculated frame-by-frame, then smoothed with a 4-second window to eliminate transient spikes. The middle 50% of the track establishes the body median — the reference energy level.

3

Find Energy Crossings

Mix In: Scan from the start — find the first moment energy stays above 70% of body median for 8 consecutive beats. That's where the track's energy commits.
Mix Out: Scan backward from the end — find where energy last sustains above the threshold before dropping into the outro.

4

Snap to Beat Grid

Raw positions are quantized to the nearest beat using the track's detected BPM. Mix-in is clamped to the first third, mix-out to the last third — no false positives in the body.

5

Score Confidence

Each point gets a confidence score (0–1) based on the energy contrast at the boundary. Sharp transitions = high confidence. Flat or ambiguous tracks fall back to 16 beats from start/end.

6

Write Everywhere

Mix points are stored as TXXX ID3 tags on the MP3, as POSITION_MARK hot cues in the Rekordbox XML, and in the local .library.json for the set builder.

Usage

Two Ways to Enable

Toggle it in the interactive wizard or fire it from the command line.

Interactive Wizard
$ pconv

Spotify playlist URL: https://open.spotify.com/playlist/3cEYp...
Target DJ software: 1 (Rekordbox)
Analyze BPM & key? Yes
Detect mix-in/mix-out points? Yes (hot cues for Rekordbox)

┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ URL: open.spotify.com/... │
│ Target: rekordbox │
│ Quality: 192k mp3 │
│ Analyze: yes + mix points │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
Command Line
# Download + analyze + detect mix points
$ pconv download --analyze-mix-points https://open.spotify.com/playlist/3cEYp...

# Full options
$ pconv download --analyze-mix-points -v -t rekordbox URL
Tuning

Configuration

All constants live in config.py. Tweak these to match your genre and mixing style.

ENERGY_THRESHOLD
0.70

Fraction of body median energy needed to consider a section "active". Lower it for ambient/minimal tracks, raise for EDM.

SUSTAIN_BEATS
8

How many consecutive beats must stay above threshold. Prevents false triggers from one-off hits or fills.

SMOOTHING_SECONDS
4.0

RMS smoothing window. Larger values ignore short breaks in energy. Smaller catches rapid buildups.

MIN_TRACK_DURATION
60s

Tracks shorter than this are skipped. Interludes, intros, and skits don't need mix points.

FALLBACK_BEATS
16

When energy is flat or detection fails, default to 16 beats from start/end. Safe for most 4/4 music.

POSITION_LIMITS
33% / 67%

Mix-in is clamped to the first third of the track, mix-out to the last third. Prevents mid-track false positives.
