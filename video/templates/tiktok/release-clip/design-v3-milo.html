RELEASE CLIP V3 — MILØ

Design reference for Milø release clips. Shared template at video/templates/tiktok/release-clip/. Output to video/output/milo/tiktok/release-clip/<serie>/.

Brand difference from DJ Gianni: Milø clips are cooler, more composed. Less slam, more groove. Cyan replaces coral. English-first copy. The music carries the energy — effects support but don't dominate. Think: Franky Rizardo set clip, not a birthday party promo.
