Design Principles

One Brand

Pic Your Booth is one premium entertainment brand. Photobooth and DJ pages are rooms in the same venue — same typography, same dark base, same components.

Colors Differentiate

Section accent colors are the only differentiator. Gold for Magic Mirror, coral for iPad, amber for Gianni, blue for Milo. The brand shell stays constant.

Same Builder, Every Product

Every product page uses the same package builder structure: locked base package, toggle upgrades, running total, single CTA. Consistent UX across services.
