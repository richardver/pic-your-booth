Design Principles
◆

Cinematic Dark

Deep charcoal backgrounds evoke the atmosphere of a dark event venue. Film grain and radial glows add depth and drama. Everything feels like a scene, not a webpage.

★

Premium First

Generous whitespace, elegant serif typography, and warm shadows communicate luxury. Every detail — from micro-animations to card spacing — must feel curated and intentional.

●

Gold for VIP Only

Gold is reserved for premium markers, accents, and pricing. It never appears on action buttons. Coral red drives action; gold signals exclusivity. This distinction is sacred.
