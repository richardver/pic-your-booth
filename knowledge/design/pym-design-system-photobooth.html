Pic Your Booth

Premium event entertainment brand. Design system, component library, and page mockups for a VIP-level photobooth experience.

01

Design Principles

Every design decision reinforces one idea: Pic Your Booth delivers premium event entertainment that feels VIP, elevated, and effortless.

01

Premium First

Everything communicates quality. Generous whitespace, warm tones, confident typography. The brand should feel like walking into a VIP event — not browsing a rental catalogue.

02

One Brand, Tiered Access

Magic Mirror XL is the flagship experience. iPad Photobooth is the accessible entry point into the premium world. Same brand DNA, different scale of experience.

03

Cinematic Warmth

Warm off-whites, soft shadows, gold accents. The palette evokes high-end event photography — never clinical or cold. Every screen should feel inviting and experiential.

02
