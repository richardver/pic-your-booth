05 — Design Rules

01
"GIANNI SESSIONS" branding never changes — builds series recognition across all volumes.
02
Gold (#FFD700) is always present as the signature constant. Logo, tagline, and accents stay gold on every release.
03
Only three things change per release: genre subtitle, volume number, and genre accent color.
04
"Volle Energie" tagline appears on every cover — the verbal brand hook.
05
Use real artist photo (B&W studio style) for recognition. Never use AI-generated faces.
06
G hexagon logo on every cover. Top-left or bottom-right corner placement.
07
Film grain / analog texture is always present — ties the visual language to vinyl and DJ culture.
08
Background pattern style adapts to genre: geometric for Afro, grid for Meezingers, glow for Dance, organic for Summer.
