Milø chose the **Clean & Bright** visual approach (2026-03-30) after reviewing 5 release clip variants.

**Why:** The clean bright variant lets footage breathe, feels effortless and precise — matches Milø's "cool, composed" personality better than the dark/moody/underwater direction.

**How to apply:**
- All Milø photo/video content: near-original colors + light teal tint, brightness(0.95) contrast(1.12) saturate(0.85)
- No heavy dark grades, no vignettes, no tunnel gradients, no moody underwater aesthetic
- Soft even lighting, natural light preferred
- Film grain max 4%, clean is default
- Teal wash: rgba(52,211,153,0.06) overlay only
- Brand materials (covers, UI) still use #050508 void background — that's a brand color, not photography direction
- Cyan #34d399 primary, diamond mark, Bebas Neue + Space Grotesk unchanged
- Mood keywords: Clean, Bright, Precise, Effortless, Groove, Open
