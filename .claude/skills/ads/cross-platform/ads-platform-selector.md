# Ads Platform Selector

Choose the right advertising platform for your business based on goals, audience, budget, and product type.

## When to Use

- Starting paid advertising for the first time
- Expanding to new platforms
- Reallocating budget across platforms
- Advising clients on platform strategy
- Evaluating platform performance

## Input Requirements

Provide:
1. **Business Type**: B2B, B2C, D2C, Local, SaaS, E-commerce, etc.
2. **Product/Service**: What you're selling
3. **Target Audience**: Demographics, interests, behaviors
4. **Goals**: Awareness, leads, sales, app installs
5. **Budget**: Monthly ad spend available
6. **Current State**: Any existing advertising

## Platform Overview

### Google Ads
**Best For**: High-intent searches, proven demand
**Audience**: People actively searching for solutions
**Minimum Budget**: $1,000-2,000/month
**Learning Curve**: Medium-High

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Captures existing demand | Doesn't create demand |
| High intent = high conversion | Can be expensive (competitive) |
| Massive reach | Complex to master |
| Multiple formats | Requires search volume |

**Ideal For**:
- Products/services people search for
- Local businesses
- E-commerce with known products
- B2B with defined solutions

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
**Best For**: Creating demand, visual products, broad targeting
**Audience**: 3B+ users, interest-based targeting
**Minimum Budget**: $500-1,500/month
**Learning Curve**: Medium

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Creates demand | iOS 14.5 tracking issues |
| Visual storytelling | Requires strong creative |
| Detailed targeting | Can't capture search intent |
| Lower CPCs than Google | Algorithm dependence |

**Ideal For**:
- D2C/e-commerce brands
- Visual products
- Impulse purchases
- Brand building
- Retargeting

### LinkedIn Ads
**Best For**: B2B, professional services, recruiting
**Audience**: 900M+ professionals
**Minimum Budget**: $3,000-5,000/month
**Learning Curve**: Medium

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Best B2B targeting | Expensive (high CPCs) |
| Professional context | Smaller audience |
| Decision-maker access | Less creative formats |
| High-value leads | Longer sales cycles |

**Ideal For**:
- B2B SaaS
- Professional services
- Enterprise sales
- Recruiting
- High-ticket B2B

### TikTok Ads
**Best For**: Gen Z/Millennials, viral potential, awareness
**Audience**: 1B+ users, skews younger
**Minimum Budget**: $500-1,500/month
**Learning Curve**: Medium

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Massive reach (young) | Older demos harder |
| Viral potential | Requires native content |
| Lower CPMs | Less proven for conversions |
| High engagement | Brand safety concerns |

**Ideal For**:
- Youth-focused brands
- Trendy/viral products
- App installs
- Brand awareness
- Entertainment/lifestyle

### Pinterest Ads
**Best For**: Visual discovery, planning-oriented purchases
**Audience**: 450M+ users, 60%+ female
**Minimum Budget**: $500-1,500/month
**Learning Curve**: Low-Medium

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| High purchase intent | Smaller audience |
| Long content lifespan | Skews female |
| Visual discovery | Limited targeting |
| Lower competition | Fewer ad formats |

**Ideal For**:
- Home decor
- Fashion/beauty
- Wedding industry
- DIY/crafts
- Food/recipes

### Amazon Ads
**Best For**: E-commerce, products sold on Amazon
**Audience**: 300M+ active customers
**Minimum Budget**: $1,000-2,000/month
**Learning Curve**: Medium

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Highest purchase intent | Amazon sellers only |
| Point of purchase | Competitive |
| Massive e-commerce reach | Less brand building |
| Product-focused | Margin pressure |

**Ideal For**:
- Amazon sellers
- Physical products
- Competitive categories
- Brand defense

### YouTube Ads
**Best For**: Video storytelling, awareness, consideration
**Audience**: 2B+ users
**Minimum Budget**: $1,500-3,000/month
**Learning Curve**: Medium-High

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Video impact | Requires video content |
| Google integration | Higher production cost |
| Targeting options | Skippable = wasted spend |
| Brand building | Longer attribution |

**Ideal For**:
- Brand awareness
- Product demonstrations
- Educational content
- Retargeting
- App promotion

### Twitter/X Ads
**Best For**: Real-time, news, B2B tech, crypto
**Audience**: 400M+ users
**Minimum Budget**: $500-1,500/month
**Learning Curve**: Low-Medium

| Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|
| Real-time targeting | Smaller audience |
| Tech-savvy users | Brand safety issues |
| Lower competition | Less targeting options |
| B2B potential | Uncertain future |

**Ideal For**:
- Tech/SaaS
- News/media
- Crypto/finance
- Event promotion
- B2B thought leadership

## Decision Framework

### Step 1: Define Primary Goal

| Goal | Best Platforms |
|------|----------------|
| Brand Awareness | Meta, TikTok, YouTube |
| Lead Generation | Google, LinkedIn, Meta |
| E-commerce Sales | Google Shopping, Meta, Amazon |
| App Installs | Meta, TikTok, Google UAC |
| Local Customers | Google, Meta |
| B2B Leads | LinkedIn, Google, Meta |

### Step 2: Match Audience

| Audience | Best Platforms |
|----------|----------------|
| Gen Z (18-24) | TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat |
| Millennials (25-40) | Meta, TikTok, Google |
| Gen X (41-56) | Meta, Google, YouTube |
| Boomers (57+) | Facebook, Google, YouTube |
| B2B Decision Makers | LinkedIn, Google |
| High Income | LinkedIn, Google, Pinterest |
| Parents | Meta, Pinterest, YouTube |

### Step 3: Consider Product Type

| Product Type | Best Platforms |
|--------------|----------------|
| Visual/lifestyle | Meta, Pinterest, TikTok |
| Technical/B2B | LinkedIn, Google |
| Impulse purchase | Meta, TikTok |
| Considered purchase | Google, YouTube |
| Local service | Google, Meta |
| Subscription/SaaS | Google, LinkedIn, Meta |

### Step 4: Budget Reality Check

| Monthly Budget | Recommended Approach |
|----------------|---------------------|
| $500-1,500 | Single platform focus |
| $1,500-5,000 | Primary + retargeting platform |
| $5,000-15,000 | 2-3 platforms |
| $15,000+ | Multi-platform strategy |

## Quick Reference: Industry Recommendations

### E-commerce/D2C
1. **Meta Ads** (primary) - demand creation
2. **Google Shopping** - capture intent
3. **TikTok** - younger audience

### B2B SaaS
1. **Google Ads** (primary) - search intent
2. **LinkedIn** - decision makers
3. **Meta** - retargeting

### Local Service Business
1. **Google Ads** (primary) - local search
2. **Meta** - local awareness
3. **Nextdoor** - neighborhood reach

### Mobile App
1. **Meta** (primary) - scale
2. **TikTok** - younger users
3. **Google UAC** - search + display

### Professional Services
1. **Google Ads** (primary) - search intent
2. **LinkedIn** - B2B clients
3. **Meta** - retargeting

## Questions to Ask

1. What does a successful customer look like?
2. How do customers currently find you?
3. What's your average customer value?
4. How long is your sales cycle?
5. What visual assets do you have?
6. Any platforms you've tried before?
