# Ads Funnel Builder

Design complete advertising funnels that turn cold audiences into customers.

## When to Use

- Building a new ad strategy from scratch
- Current campaigns aren't scaling
- High traffic but low conversions
- Need to reduce customer acquisition cost
- Creating a retargeting strategy
- Expanding beyond single-campaign approach

## Input Requirements

Provide:
1. **Business Model**: E-commerce, SaaS, Lead Gen, Local, etc.
2. **Product/Service**: What you're selling
3. **Price Point**: Affects funnel complexity
4. **Sales Cycle**: Impulse → Considered purchase length
5. **Current Ads**: What you're running now
6. **Budget**: Monthly ad spend
7. **Platforms**: Google, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.

## Funnel Framework Overview

```
                    AWARENESS (TOFU)
                         ↓
        Cold audiences who don't know you
              Reach & engagement focused
                         ↓
                  CONSIDERATION (MOFU)
                         ↓
        Warm audiences who showed interest
            Education & trust focused
                         ↓
                   CONVERSION (BOFU)
                         ↓
        Hot audiences ready to buy
              Direct response focused
                         ↓
                     RETENTION
                         ↓
        Existing customers
            Upsell & loyalty focused
```

## Funnel Stage Details

### Stage 1: Top of Funnel (TOFU) - Awareness

**Goal**: Introduce brand to new audiences

**Audience**:
- Lookalikes of customers
- Interest-based targeting
- Broad targeting (with good creative)
- Cold traffic

**Content Types**:
- Educational content
- Entertaining/viral content
- Brand story videos
- Problem awareness content
- Industry insights

**Metrics That Matter**:
- Reach
- CPM
- Video views (ThruPlay)
- Engagement rate
- Landing page views

**Budget Allocation**: 40-60% for scaling, 20-30% for mature accounts

**Example Campaign Types**:

| Platform | Campaign Type | Objective |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| Meta | Video Views | ThruPlay |
| Meta | Reach | Brand Awareness |
| Google | YouTube | Video Views |
| Google | Display | Awareness |
| TikTok | Reach | Video Views |

### Stage 2: Middle of Funnel (MOFU) - Consideration

**Goal**: Build trust and educate engaged prospects

**Audience**:
- Website visitors (all pages)
- Video viewers (50%+)
- Social engagers
- Email subscribers (non-purchasers)
- Add to cart (not purchased)

**Content Types**:
- Product demonstrations
- Customer testimonials
- Case studies
- Comparison content
- FAQ/objection handling
- Free trials/samples

**Metrics That Matter**:
- CTR
- Cost per landing page view
- Time on site
- Email signups
- Content engagement

**Budget Allocation**: 20-30%

**Example Campaign Types**:

| Platform | Campaign Type | Objective |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| Meta | Traffic | Landing Page Views |
| Meta | Engagement | Post Engagement |
| Google | Search | Non-brand keywords |
| Google | YouTube | Consideration |
| LinkedIn | Lead Gen | Content downloads |

### Stage 3: Bottom of Funnel (BOFU) - Conversion

**Goal**: Convert high-intent prospects

**Audience**:
- Product page viewers (7-14 days)
- Add to cart (7-14 days)
- Checkout started (7 days)
- High-engagement website visitors
- Pricing page viewers

**Content Types**:
- Direct response offers
- Limited-time promotions
- Free shipping reminders
- Abandoned cart reminders
- Testimonial + CTA
- Guarantee/risk reversal

**Metrics That Matter**:
- Conversion rate
- CPA / ROAS
- Cost per purchase
- Revenue

**Budget Allocation**: 20-40%

**Example Campaign Types**:

| Platform | Campaign Type | Objective |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| Meta | Conversions | Purchase |
| Meta | Catalog Sales | DPA Retargeting |
| Google | Search | Brand + high-intent |
| Google | Shopping | ROAS target |
| Google | PMax | Conversions |

### Stage 4: Retention - Post-Purchase

**Goal**: Increase LTV, generate repeat purchases

**Audience**:
- Past purchasers
- Subscription cancellations
- Lapsed customers (90-180 days)
- High-LTV customers

**Content Types**:
- New product announcements
- Cross-sell/upsell offers
- Loyalty rewards
- Referral programs
- Replenishment reminders

**Metrics That Matter**:
- Repeat purchase rate
- Customer LTV
- Referral rate
- ROAS on retention campaigns

**Budget Allocation**: 10-20%

## Funnel Templates by Business Type

### E-commerce Funnel
- TOFU: Video views, brand awareness
- MOFU: Traffic to product pages, email capture
- BOFU: Dynamic product ads, cart abandonment
- Retention: New arrivals, replenishment

### SaaS Funnel
- TOFU: Educational content, industry insights
- MOFU: Free trial signup, demo booking
- BOFU: Trial to paid conversion
- Retention: Upsell, churn prevention

### Lead Gen Funnel
- TOFU: Thought leadership, problem awareness
- MOFU: Lead magnet, webinar registration
- BOFU: Consultation booking, quote request
- Retention: Referral program, upsell

### Local Business Funnel
- TOFU: Local awareness, store visits
- MOFU: Offer redemption, appointment booking
- BOFU: Call/direction clicks
- Retention: Loyalty, reviews

## Common Funnel Mistakes

1. **Only running BOFU**: No new audiences entering funnel
2. **No exclusions**: Showing same ads to everyone
3. **Wrong metrics per stage**: Judging TOFU on conversions
4. **Audience overlap**: Campaigns competing against each other
5. **Creative mismatch**: Same creative at every stage

## Questions to Ask

1. What's your average order value/deal size?
2. How long does it take someone to decide?
3. What content do you already have?
4. What audiences are you currently targeting?
5. What's working now that we should scale?
6. What's your customer lifetime value?
