# Social Creatives - Platform-by-Platform Guide

**PYB actieve platformen: Instagram + TikTok + YouTube Shorts.** LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube long-form secties hieronder zijn generieke referentie — niet actief voor PYB.

---

## Instagram

### Feed vs Stories vs Reels Strategy
| Format | Purpose | Cadence |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Feed (carousel/image) | Portfolio, testimonials, brand building | 3-4x/week |
| Stories | Behind-the-scenes, polls, daily engagement | Daily |
| Reels | Reach, discovery, viral content | 3-5x/week |

### Caption Formula
**Hook → Value → CTA → Hashtags**
1. Opening line that stops the scroll (question, bold claim, or story)
2. 2-3 lines of value or context
3. Clear CTA (save, share, comment, link in bio)
4. 20-30 relevant hashtags (mix of small, medium, large)

### Hashtag Tiers
| Tier | Size | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| Niche | <10K posts | Highly targeted, less competition |
| Medium | 10K-500K | Good discovery, moderate competition |
| Large | 500K-5M | Broad reach, high competition |
| Mega | >5M | Awareness only, very high competition |

Use 10 niche, 10 medium, 5 large, 5 mega as a starting mix.

## TikTok

### Hook Techniques (First 1-3 Seconds)
1. **Text hook on screen**: Bold statement before speaking
2. **Direct address**: "Here's something nobody tells you about..."
3. **Pattern interrupt**: Unexpected visual or action
4. **Question**: "Did you know...?"
5. **Trending sound**: Leverage current audio trends

### Content Format Templates
| Format | Structure | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Tutorial | Problem → Step-by-step → Result | Education, how-to |
| Before/After | Setup → Transformation → Reveal | Results, visual impact |
| Day-in-the-life | Morning → Activity → Highlight | Personal brand, BTS |
| Trending sound | Match audio beat to visual cuts | Discovery, reach |
| Duet/Stitch | React to popular content | Engagement, community |

### TikTok Best Practices
- Hook in first 1 second
- Native feel (not overproduced)
- Text overlays > voiceover for first impression
- Vertical only (9:16)
- 15-60 seconds optimal
- Use trending sounds
- Post 1-3x daily for growth

## LinkedIn

### Post Types & Performance
| Type | Avg Engagement | Best For |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Text-only (with line breaks) | High | Thought leadership, stories |
| Carousel (PDF document) | Very High | Frameworks, step-by-step |
| Poll | High | Engagement, research |
| Video (native) | Medium-High | Expert commentary, BTS |
| Image + text | Medium | Data visualizations, quotes |

### Hook Formulas
1. **Bold claim**: "Most marketing teams waste 60% of their ad budget. Here's why:"
2. **Personal story**: "I was fired from my first marketing job. Best thing that ever happened."
3. **Contrarian take**: "Unpopular opinion: SEO is not dead. Your strategy is."
4. **Data drop**: "We analyzed 10,000 LinkedIn posts. Here's what gets engagement:"
5. **Question**: "What's the one metric you'd save if you could only track one?"

### Optimal Length
1,200-1,500 characters. Line breaks every 1-2 sentences. 3-5 hashtags.

## Video Scripting Reference

### Video Script Structures

**AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)**
- Attention: Hook that stops the scroll
- Interest: Present the problem or opportunity
- Desire: Show the solution and its benefits
- Action: Clear CTA

**PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)**
- Problem: State the pain point
- Agitate: Make the pain feel urgent
- Solution: Present your offer

**Before-After-Bridge**
- Before: Current painful state
- After: Desired future state
- Bridge: How your product/service gets them there

### 12 Proven Hook Formulas
1. "Stop [doing thing everyone does]"
2. "The secret to [desired outcome] is..."
3. "I used to [relatable struggle]. Then I discovered..."
4. "What if I told you [surprising claim]?"
5. "This is the biggest mistake [audience] makes"
6. "Here's why [common belief] is wrong"
7. "[Number] things I wish I knew about [topic]"
8. "Nobody talks about [hidden truth]"
9. "The [industry] doesn't want you to know this"
10. "I tested [thing] for [timeframe]. Here's what happened"
11. "In [time], you'll know exactly how to [outcome]"
12. "POV: You just [achieved desired outcome]"

### Video Length by Platform
| Platform | Optimal Length | Max |
|----------|---------------|-----|
| TikTok | 15-60s | 10 min |
| Instagram Reels | 15-30s | 90s |
| Instagram Stories | 15s per slide | 60s |
| Facebook Feed | 30-120s | 240 min |
| YouTube Shorts | 30-60s | 60s |
| YouTube | 8-15 min | 12 hours |

### Visual Direction Notes
- **Camera vocabulary**: Wide (context), Medium (action), Close-up (emotion/detail), POV (immersion)
- **B-roll cues**: "Cut to [visual] showing [concept]"
- **On-screen text timing**: Appear 0.5s before spoken word, hold 2s minimum
- **Music mood mapping**: Match energy to content phase (upbeat for hooks, softer for story, building for CTA)
