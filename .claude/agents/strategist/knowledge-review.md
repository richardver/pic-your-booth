# Brand Review

How to review content for brand compliance. Used by `/brand-audit` skill.

---

## Flagging Severity

| Level | When to Flag |
|---|---|
| **CRITICAL** | Contradicts brand values, wrong pricing, wrong product names |
| **WARNING** | Tone mismatch, missing differentiator, sounds generic |
| **INFO** | Opportunity to strengthen brand message |

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Matches the correct segment tone (premium / accessible / energy)
- [ ] Includes a clear value proposition
- [ ] CTA is specific and action-oriented
- [ ] No jargon or unnecessary complexity
- [ ] Feels energetic and confident, not desperate
- [ ] Highlights PYB differentiators (DSLR quality, DJ combo, Spotify optimization, family energy)
- [ ] Dutch copy sounds natural (not translated-from-English)
- [ ] Visual direction matches brand (professional but fun, not stock-photo generic)
- [ ] Charm pricing used (€199, €599, €149)
- [ ] No red (#ff494a) or pink (#e839a0) colors
- [ ] Party Booth (not iPad Photobooth)
- [ ] PicYourBooth (not Pic Your Moment)
- [ ] Gold buttons have black text

## Key Messages to Protect

- "Niet zomaar een booth — een ervaring" (Magic Mirror)
- "Foto plezier voor elk feest" (Party Booth)
- "Eén team, één vibe, één boeking" (DJ + Photobooth combo)
- Spotify playlist optimization as unique differentiator
- Family business energy — genuine, personal, passionate

## Segments — Tone Map

| Segment | Tone | Emphasize |
|---|---|---|
| Magic Mirror XL | Polished, premium, trust | Quality, experience, DSLR, 5 uur inclusief |
| Party Booth | Casual, fun, accessible | Ease, value, fun factor |
| DJ Services | Energetic, personal | Vibe, uniqueness, Spotify |

## Output Format

1. **Pass / Revise / Reject** — overall verdict
2. **Brand alignment score** (1-10)
3. **Specific feedback** — what to fix, with suggested alternatives
4. **Opportunities** — ways to strengthen the brand message
