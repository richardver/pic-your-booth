# Landing Pages — Structure, Copy & Conversion

Generic landing page frameworks. PYB-specific page templates live in the designer agent's `knowledge-website.md`.

---

## Landing Page Structure Template

### Standard Conversion Page
```
[1. Hero Section]          Above the fold — headline, subheadline, CTA, hero image
[2. Social Proof Bar]      Logos, "Trusted by X", star ratings
[3. Problem/Pain Section]  Agitate the pain point
[4. Solution/Features]     How you solve it (3-5 benefits)
[5. Detailed Social Proof] Testimonials, case studies, metrics
[6. How It Works]          3-step process
[7. More Benefits]         Deeper feature details
[8. FAQ]                   Address objections
[9. Final CTA Section]     Repeat offer + CTA button
```

---

## Hero Section Formulas

### Formula 1: Outcome-First
- **Headline**: "[Achieve specific outcome] without [common pain]"
- **Subheadline**: "[Product] helps [audience] [benefit] by [how]"

### Formula 2: Audience-First
- **Headline**: "The [category] built for [specific audience]"
- **Subheadline**: "[Feature] + [Feature] + [Feature] in one place"

### Formula 3: Social Proof-First
- **Headline**: "Join [number]+ [audience] who [achieved outcome]"
- **Subheadline**: "[Product] is the #1 rated [category] for [use case]"

### Formula 4: Pain-First
- **Headline**: "Stop [painful activity]. Start [desired outcome]."
- **Subheadline**: "[Product] replaces [old way] with [better way]"

## Above-the-Fold Checklist

- [ ] Visitor understands what you offer in 5 seconds
- [ ] Headline is clear and specific (not clever/vague)
- [ ] Subheadline supports with detail
- [ ] ONE clear CTA visible without scrolling
- [ ] CTA button is high-contrast and action-oriented
- [ ] Relevant hero image or product visual
- [ ] No navigation distractions

---

## CTA Copy Formula

```
[Action Verb] + [Value/Outcome] + [Optional: Time/Ease Qualifier]
```

Examples:
- "Get My Quote" (better than "Submit")
- "See Pricing Plans" (better than "Learn More")
- "Book a 15-Min Demo" (better than "Contact Us")

### CTA Button Best Practices
- High-contrast color (stands out from page)
- Minimum 44px height for mobile
- White space around the button
- Repeat CTA at top and bottom (minimum)
- Sub-CTA text to reduce anxiety

## Social Proof Types & Placement

| Type | Best Placement | Impact |
|------|---------------|--------|
| Customer logos | Below hero | High |
| Specific metrics | Hero or near CTA | Very High |
| Testimonial quotes | After features | High |
| Video testimonials | Dedicated section | Very High |
| Star ratings | Near CTA | High |
| Trust badges | Near form/checkout | Medium |

---

## Message Match Principles

The landing page must match the referral source:

| Source | Must Match |
|--------|-----------|
| Paid ad | Headline echoes ad, offer matches promise |
| Email | Headline references subject/offer |
| Organic search | H1 matches search intent |

Mismatch = high bounce rate.
