# Google Ads

Google Ads strategy for PYB — Search, Shopping, Performance Max, Display.

---

## Why Google Ads for PYB

- Captures **intent** — people searching "photobooth huren" are ready to book
- Meta Ads captures attention (push), Google captures demand (pull)
- Together they cover the full funnel: awareness (Meta) → intent (Google)

## Campaign Types for PYB

### Search Campaigns (Primary)
- **Brand campaigns**: "picyourbooth", "pic your booth" — protect brand, cheap clicks
- **Generic campaigns**: "photobooth huren", "fotobooth bruiloft", "dj huren randstad"
- **Long-tail**: "premium photobooth bruiloft randstad", "dj en fotobooth huren"

### Performance Max (When Ready)
- Needs 30+ conversions in 30 days for ML to work
- Asset groups by segment (Magic Mirror, Party Booth, DJ)
- Use after Search campaigns prove conversion

### Display/YouTube (Future)
- Retargeting website visitors
- Video ads from event highlight footage
