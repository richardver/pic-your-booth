## Pricing Rules

1. **All prices include 21% BTW** — never quote ex-BTW
2. **Charm pricing** — use €199/€599/€149, not round numbers
3. **Add-ons use charm pricing too** — €24, €39, €49, €149, €499
4. **Show "vanaf" pricing** — "Vanaf €199" / "Vanaf €599" / "Vanaf €149"
5. **Package builder on website** — shows running total including BTW
6. **No hidden costs** — price on website = price on invoice
7. **"MEEST GEKOZEN" badge** — on Magic Mirror XL (homepage service cards)

## Anchoring Strategy

- On homepage: show Magic Mirror XL (€599) in the middle with "MEEST GEKOZEN" badge
- On product pages: show most expensive upgrade first in the builder
- In proposals: recommend the VIP upgrade as "Aanbevolen"
- Compare to competitors: "Een DJ bureau rekent €500+, bij ons vanaf €149 erbij"
