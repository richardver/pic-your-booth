# Party Booth

> Foto plezier voor elk feest.

Compact, digital-only photobooth. Customer picks it up, sets it up themselves, and enjoys. Accessible entry point — fun for every budget.

## What It Is

- Digital photobooth (tablet-based)
- Customer self-service: ophalen bij ons, zelf opzetten, 5 minuten klaar
- Digital photo strips sent to guests' phones
- No on-site operator required (unless booked as upgrade)

## What's Included (Base Package — €199)

- Party Booth hardware (ophalen bij ons)
- Basis fotostrip design
- Digital photo delivery (guests scan QR → get photos on phone)
- Setup instructions
