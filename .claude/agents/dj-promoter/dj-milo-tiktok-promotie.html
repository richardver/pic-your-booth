Promotion Playbook

TIKTOK PROMOTION

From SoundCloud mix to TikTok views to bookings. Clip-by-clip scripts, storyboards, and caption templates. Repeatable system for every release.

The System

EVERY MIX = 7 CLIPS

Every SoundCloud mix release gets a 7-day promotion cycle on TikTok. 7 clips that each highlight a different aspect of the set. Film everything during the recording session and at live gigs — that is the most authentic content.
WHY 7 DAYS?

TikTok's algorithm rewards consistent posting. 7 clips in 7 days gives the algorithm enough data to push you. After day 3-4, TikTok knows who wants to see your content.

FILM AT GIGS + SESSIONS

Phone on a tripod behind or beside the controller. Film the whole session. Cut the best moments later. GetFunky sets, home sessions, soundchecks — all usable material.

ALWAYS LINK TO SET

Every clip is an ad for the full set on SoundCloud. "link in bio" or "full set on soundcloud" in every caption. Never a clip without a redirect.

EDIT WITH REMOTION

React-based video templates. Define each clip as a reusable component with Milø's exact design tokens (cyan, Bebas Neue, sine wave, diamond). Same data, new genre = new clip. Export at 1080x1920 (9:16).

Golden Rule: Hook in 1 Second

TikTok users scroll past your video in 0.3 seconds. Your first frame must be action: hands on the mixer, a bass drop, text on screen. Never start with "hey guys" or a still image. The music IS the hook. Keep it minimal — one word, one vibe, one moment.

The Age Reveal Hook

Milø's age is a genuine differentiator. "16 and already on the decks at [venue]" consistently drives high engagement because it breaks expectations. Use this hook once per month maximum — keep it powerful, never overuse it. Pair it with impressive footage to back up the claim.

Discoverability

HASHTAG STRATEGY

TikTok hashtags work differently than Instagram. Use 5-10 targeted tags, not 30 broad ones. Mix niche (small reach, high relevance) and broad (large reach, more competition). English-first for Milø's international audience.

TikTok vs Instagram Hashtag Rules

TikTok: 5-10 hashtags. Less is more. TikTok reads your video content (audio, text, visuals) to categorize — hashtags are an aid, not the only method.

Instagram: 20-30 hashtags. More is more. Instagram leans heavier on hashtags for discovery. Add broader tags (#dj, #music, #netherlands, #nightlife) alongside the niche tags.

Video Tooling

REMOTION WORKFLOW

We use Remotion instead of CapCut. Remotion is a React framework for programmatic videos. That means: make a template once, use it for every release with different data. Consistent, scalable, on-brand.
WHY REMOTION?

Reusable templates: Make a "Mix Drop" component once with Milø's design tokens (cyan, Bebas Neue, sine wave, diamond). Next time: same template, different genre and tracks. No manual editing.

BRAND CONSISTENCY

Every clip uses the exact same colors, fonts, and animations as the cover art and website. No "close enough" — pixel-perfect Milø, every time.

TIKTOK TEMPLATE

Remotion has a dedicated TikTok starter: npx create-video@latest --tiktok. 9:16 format, word-by-word captions via Whisper.cpp, and Tailwind CSS support out of the box.

RENDER & EXPORT

Render via CLI (npx remotion render), in the browser (Remotion Studio), or server-side. Exports directly to MP4. No watermarks, full control over quality.

CLIP TEMPLATES AS REACT COMPONENTS

Each clip type becomes a reusable React component. Change the props (genre, tracklist, colors) and you have a new clip. Here are the 7 templates you need:
Component Props Animations Duration
<TeaserClip /> genre, seriesName, coverArt, audioSnippet Fade-in text, cover art zoom, sine wave pulse, diamond reveal 10-15s (300-450 frames)
<MixDropClip /> genre, seriesName, videoFile, dropTimestamp Text overlay, speed ramp on drop, diamond burst, fade-to-cover 15-30s (450-900 frames)
<TransitionClip /> genre, videoFile, hookText Text hook animation, pull-back reveal, sine wave overlay 15-20s (450-600 frames)
<TrackRevealClip /> genre, videoFile, trackArtist, trackName Text build, track ID reveal, diamond transition 10-15s (300-450 frames)
<TracklistClip /> genre, seriesName, tracks[], accentColor Scroll-up tracklist, cyan numbering, staggered appear 20-30s (600-900 frames)

Design Tokens in Remotion

Use Milø's design tokens as CSS variables or a shared config in your Remotion project:

--mi: #34d399 (cyan/Tech House), --blue: #38bdf8 (House), --violet: #8b5cf6 (Deep)
font-family: 'Bebas Neue' (titles), 'Space Grotesk' (body)

The accent color is determined by the genre prop. Same component works for all three series. Diamond shapes replace hexagons, sine waves replace EQ bars.

Behind-the-Decks Footage + Remotion Overlay

The workflow is hybrid: film the behind-the-decks footage with your phone (raw video), import into Remotion as <OffthreadVideo />, and add the branded text overlays, sine waves, and cover art programmatically. This combines authentic footage with pixel-perfect branding.

Production

FILMING SETUP

You don't need more than a phone and a tripod. Here is exactly how to film during sets and sessions so you have enough material for all 7 clips.
CAMERA SETUP

Position 1 (main): Phone on tripod, behind or beside you. Aimed at your hands + controller. This is your "behind the decks" angle — 80% of your clips come from here. Dark, moody, close.

SECOND ANGLE (OPTIONAL)

Position 2: Second phone or GoPro on the table, low angle looking up. Gives variety in your edits. Not required for the first mixes, but makes the content stronger.

LIGHTING

Dark room + 1 light source (LED strip, cyan/blue gel or smart bulb). It should feel like a nightclub, not an office. The controller can glow, you stay in shadow. Cyan/blue tints only — never warm light.

AUDIO

Film with phone audio (the controller/speakers give enough sound). In the edit: replace audio with the WAV/FLAC of the set for optimal quality. Sync on the drop.

Minimal Setup Checklist

☐ Phone (iPhone 13+ or similar)
☐ Phone tripod or gorillapod

POST TIMING

Posting 3-5x per week. Peak times for the house/electronic music audience are later in the evening — 20:00-00:00 on TikTok. The 7-day cycle can be spread across 2 weeks if needed.

GetFunky Content Gold

Every GetFunky gig is a content goldmine. Film BTS (soundcheck, setup, crowd), set clips, transitions, and venue atmosphere. One gig = multiple weeks of content. Always film the venue name/logo for location tagging.
