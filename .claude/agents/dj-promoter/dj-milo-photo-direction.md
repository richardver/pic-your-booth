# Milø - Profile Photo Direction

Guidance for Milø profile photography across all platforms and brand materials.

**Visual approach: Clean & Bright** — let the footage and photos breathe. Near-original colors with a light teal tint. No heavy dark grades, no moody underwater aesthetic.

---

## Style

- Clean, bright studio portrait — natural light or soft even lighting
- Light teal/cyan wash over near-original colors — subtle, never heavy
- Airy backgrounds with depth — open, not dark or confined
- Bright, effortless, precise — cinematic but not moody
- High brightness, gentle contrast boost, slight desaturation (0.85)
- No B&W base, no heavy color grade overlays — keep it natural

## Wardrobe

- Headphones on or around neck
- Dark clothing — black or very dark (contrast against bright setting)
- Clean, minimal styling

## Crop & Usage

- Clean crop for profile picture use across all platforms
- Square-safe composition (1:1)
- Works at small sizes (profile avatars) and large (banners, covers)
- Minimum 800x800px
- Border-radius: 24px in UI context

## Mood Keywords

Clean — Bright — Precise — Effortless — Groove — Open

## Video/Content Photography

- Color grade: near-original colors + light teal tint. brightness(0.95) contrast(1.12) saturate(0.85). Never dark/moody.
- Lighting: soft, even lighting. Natural light preferred. Subtle cyan accent. No heavy single-source dramatic lighting.
- Composition: clean, minimal. Negative space. Let the subject breathe.
- POV: behind-the-decks for set clips. Low angle for authority. Close-ups on hands/mixer.
- Grain: very subtle film grain (4% max). Clean is default.
- Overlays: light teal wash only (rgba(52,211,153,0.06) overlay). No vignettes, no tunnel gradients.
- Bottom fade: soft only, for text readability (transparent 50% to rgba(5,5,8,0.4) 100%).
