# DJ Gianni - Profile Photo Direction

Guidance for DJ Gianni profile photography across all platforms and brand materials.

---

## Style

- Black & white, studio-style close-up portrait
- Hard single light source, pure black background
- Film grain (Ilford HP5 style), sharp focus on eyes
- Cinematic, not commercial

## Wardrobe

- Headphones around neck
- Black hoodie
- Clean, minimal styling

## Crop & Usage

- Clean crop for profile picture use across all platforms
- Square-safe composition
- Works at small sizes (profile avatars) and large (banners, covers)
