SoundCloud mix playbook for Milø. Tech House, House Grooves and Deep Cuts — three series, one system.

Why Mixes

SOUNDCLOUD AS GROWTH ENGINE

Mixes are the proof that Milø delivers. No talk, no promises — just 60 minutes deep in the groove. SoundCloud is where bookings start: a host hears a set, sends the link to the organiser, and the booking is locked in.
BUILD REACH

SoundCloud has 175M+ monthly listeners. House and tech house are among the most active communities. Milø sits right at the centre of that scene.

PORTFOLIO EFFECT

Every mix is a business card. Clients want to hear how you sound before they book. Three series show versatility without losing identity.

CONTENT MACHINE

A 60-minute mix generates 5-7 TikTok clips. Each clip can go viral and drives listeners back to the full set. More views = more streams = more bookings.

Atmospheric, late-night selections. Warm pads, deep sub-bass, hypnotic textures. Josh Baker, Cassian, Rufus Du Sol (club edits). The set for after-hours and intimate settings.

Length 45-50 minutes
Tracks 10-14 tracks
BPM range 118-124 BPM
Status New — to produce
Why these lengths?

60 min (Tech House / House Grooves): Industry-standard mix length for house music. Long enough to build a journey, short enough for repeat listens. SoundCloud's algorithm rewards completion rate — 60 min is the sweet spot.
45-50 min (Deep Cuts): Atmospheric music needs fewer tracks with longer transitions. Slower build, deeper immersion. Shorter length improves replay value and completion metrics.

Branding in Audio

VOICE TAG PLACEMENT

Voice tags make a set recognisable. The key is subtlety: enough to build your brand, not so much it disrupts the listening experience. Maximum 1 tag per 4-5 minutes. Less is more.
0:00
Opener Tag
"Milø... Deep in the groove" or "This is Milø"
Right after the first few seconds of intro. Set the tone.
~10:00
Series-specific Tag
Tech House: "Milø... Tech House Sessions"
House Grooves: "House Grooves... Milø"
Deep Cuts: "Milø... Deep Cuts"
~20:00
Drop Tag
"Milø" or "Next wave"
Just before a big drop or transition. Short and punchy.
~30:00
Transition Tag
"Milø in the mix" or "Deep in the groove"
During a genre shift or energy boost in the set.
~45:00
Drop Tag (repeat)
"Next wave"
Optional for 60-min sets. Skip for shorter Deep Cuts mixes.
End
Outro Tag
"This was Milø... Deep in the groove"
or "Milø... until the next wave"
Close with the tagline. Let it linger.
Voice Tag Production

Voice: Calm, low, confident. Don't shout.
Effect: Light reverb/delay, clean. No autotune.
Length: Max 2-3 seconds per tag.
Recording: Quiet room, phone mic or USB mic, mono.
Frequency: Max 1 tag per 4-5 minutes. For a 60-min mix = maximum 8 tags including opener and outro.

Set length Number of tags Structure
45-50 min (Deep Cuts) 5-6 Opener + 3 mid-tags + outro
60 min (Tech House) 7-8 Opener + 5 mid-tags + outro

Repeatable Process

MIX PLAYBOOK

This is the step-by-step process for every mix release. Repeatable, consistent, professional. Print this out and hang it by your setup.
  1. 1

    Track Selection & Playlist Building

    Choose 10-22 tracks depending on the series. Check BPM ranges. Build a playlist to test the flow. Listen through the sequence at least 2x before recording.

    1-2 days
  2. 2

    Record the Set

    Record in a quiet space. Use your controller + laptop. Record in WAV/FLAC (lossless). Film behind-the-decks footage simultaneously with your phone on a tripod (for TikTok content).

    2-3 hours
  3. 3

    Add Voice Tags

    Add voice tags at the right moments (see Voice Tag Placement above). Opener, 3-5 mid-tags, outro. Mixdown to WAV.

    30-45 min
  4. 4

    Generate Cover Art

    Use the Gemini prompt template with the correct series colours. Generate at 3000x3000px. Check that the title, genre tags and "Deep in the Groove" are correct. Export as PNG.

    15-30 min
  5. 5

    SoundCloud Upload

    Upload WAV/FLAC. Add cover art. Write title in the correct format. Copy description template and fill in tracklist. Add all tags. Set publish time (Friday 18:00 or Saturday 12:00). Add to series playlist.

    20 min
  6. 6

    Cut TikTok Clips & Promote

    Cut 5-7 clips from the mix. Focus on the best transitions, drops, and melodic moments. Post as TikTok and Instagram Reels with SoundCloud link in bio.

    2-3 hours
  7. 7

    Publish on SoundCloud

    Release the mix on SoundCloud (Friday 18:00 or Saturday 12:00). Share in Instagram Stories. Update your SoundCloud bio if this is a new series. Repost from DJ accounts you follow to build network.

    20 min
  8. 8

    Analyse & Learn

    After 2 weeks: check SoundCloud plays, completion rate, likes, reposts. Check TikTok views and engagement. Which clip performed best? Which track got the most reactions? Adjust the next mix based on data.

    30 min (after 2 weeks)
Total time investment per mix

One-time production: ~4-6 hours (steps 1-4)
Upload & clips: ~2.5-3.5 hours (steps 5-6)
Promotion: ~35 min spread over 7 days (step 7)
Total: 7-10 hours per mix, spread over ~10 days

Community Growth

ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

SoundCloud is a community platform. Growth comes from active engagement, not just uploading and waiting. Here is the playbook for building a following.
REPOST NETWORK

Follow and repost mixes from DJs in the same scene. Build genuine connections. When you repost others, they notice and return the favour. Target 3-5 reposts per week from quality accounts.

COMMENT & ENGAGE

Leave thoughtful comments on mixes you genuinely enjoy. Timestamp comments ("That transition at 14:20 is fire") get the most engagement back. Never spam generic comments.

CLIP TEASERS

Cut 15-30 second clips from the best moments of each mix. Post on TikTok and Instagram Reels with "Full mix on SoundCloud" CTA. The algorithm favours short, high-energy clips.

Weekly Engagement Routine

Monday: Repost 1 quality mix from another DJ.
Wednesday: Comment on 3-5 mixes with genuine, timestamped feedback.
Friday: Post a TikTok/Reel teaser if a new mix is dropping.
Weekend: Share SoundCloud link in Instagram Stories after any gig.
Total: ~30 min/week
